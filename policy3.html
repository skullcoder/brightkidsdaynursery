Bright Kids Day Nursery Ltd / Bright Kids Hackney Ltd
Adopted: [Date] | To be reviewed: [Date]

Buddy System Overview

Each new staff member is assigned a buddy who will guide them through their induction period and provide ongoing support during their first few months of employment.

Buddy Responsibilities

  • Provide guidance and support during the first 3 months
  • Answer questions about procedures and policies
  • Introduce new staff to colleagues and children
  • Demonstrate best practices and routines
  • Provide feedback and encouragement
  • Escalate any concerns to management

New Staff Responsibilities

  • Ask questions and seek clarification
  • Follow buddy's guidance and advice
  • Be open to feedback and learning
  • Maintain professional communication
  • Report any issues or concerns promptly
